## 💡 Tips for Testing

1. **High Nitrogen** → Better for leafy crops (Rice, Maize, Jute)
2. **High Phosphorus** → Better for pulses and legumes
3. **High Potassium** → Better for fruits (Banana, Mango, Papaya)
4. **High Temperature** → Tropical crops (Cotton, Mango, Coconut)
5. **High Humidity** → Wet crops (Rice, Jute, Coffee)
6. **Low pH (5.5-6.5)** → Acidic soil crops (Orange, Coffee)
7. **High pH (7.0+)** → Alkaline soil crops (Chickpea, Lentil)
